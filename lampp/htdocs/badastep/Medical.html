The Top Medical Colleges in India include AIIMS New Delhi, PGIMER Chandigarh, CMC Vellore, and JIPMER Pondicherry. These colleges offer different medical courses like MBBS, BDS, MCh, BAMS, and other related medical courses. Admissions across top medical colleges in India are based on merit or entrance exams such as NEET, NEET-PG, and CUET PG. There are 2251 medical colleges in India, of which 1569 are private and 645 are government medical colleges. The fees for Top Government Medical Colleges in India can range between INR 6,500 - 1,00,000. However, for Private Medical Colleges in India, the fees can go up to as much as INR 20,00,000 - 81,00,000. Every medical student's dream is to get admission to the top medical colleges in India. Over 23 lakh students have applied for the NEET 2024 exam, but only 50% are expected to qualify, based on last year's trends. Therefore, every NEET aspirant strives to qualify for the exam to secure a seat in one of the top medical colleges in India.
