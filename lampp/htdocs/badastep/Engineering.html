The Top Engineering Colleges in India include IIT Madras, IIT Delhi, IIT Bombay, Jadavpur University, etc. There are 6207 recognised Engineering colleges in India, of which 4495 are Private and 1383 are Government Engineering colleges. The best Private Engineering colleges in India include Vellore Institute of Technology, Amrita School of Engineering, BITS Pilani, etc. Admission is based on Engineering Entrance Exams such as JEE Main, BITSAT, WBJEE, JEE Advanced, TANCET, etc conducted at the National or state level. IIT Madras is the best Engineering college in India, per NIRF rankings 2023, followed by IIT Delhi and IIT Bombay. The Top Engineering Colleges in India except IIT are Jadavpur University, Vellore Institute of Technology, NIT Surathkal, Anna University, etc. The Best Engineering Colleges in India for CSE are IIT Bombay, IIT Delhi, and BITS Pilani. Some of the Best Aerospace Engineering Colleges in India in India include IIT Bombay, MIT Manipal, SRM University Chennai, etc.
