The Top International Studies Colleges in India include Jawaharlal Nehru University (JNU), Delhi University, and Tata Institute of Social Sciences (TISS), among others. India has numerous esteemed institutions offering undergraduate and postgraduate programs in International Studies, focusing on global affairs, diplomacy, and international relations. Admission to these programs often requires clearing entrance exams such as the JNUEE or DUET. Jawaharlal Nehru University (JNU) is widely recognized as one of the best for International Studies in India, followed by Delhi University and Tata Institute of Social Sciences (TISS). These institutions are known for their robust academic programs, experienced faculty, and strong global connections, providing students with an in-depth understanding of international relations and policy.
