The top Management colleges in India include IIM Ahmedabad, IIM Bangalore, XLRI Jamshedpur, MDI Gurgaon, etc. There are around 7395 Management colleges in India which include 5974 Private and 1230 Government colleges. Top Management colleges in India are highly reputed for their courses MBA, BBA, PGDM, etc. These colleges offer courses Online, Offline as well Distance Education mode. IIM Ahmedabad is the best Management college in India and is ranked #1 by NIRF, Business Today, and The Times Ranking for 2023. The top Management colleges in India admit students through Management Entrance Exams like CAT, GMAT, XAT, MAT, SNAP, XAT, etc. followed by a GD/ PI round. Some colleges also offer direct admission. IIM Ahmedabad, Department of Management Studies IIT Delhi, and XLRI Jamshedpur are some of the top ten MBA colleges in India. The Top Management Colleges in India for Finance include IIM Bangalore, IIM Calcutta, XLRI Jamshedpur, and many others. There are around 240 Management colleges in New Delhi. The Best Management Colleges in Delhi are DMS IIT Delhi, Jamia Millia Islamia, and IIFT Delhi. The fees of various Management courses range from INR 2.50 lakh to INR 27 lakh.
